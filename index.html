Game of PIG

The Game of Pig is a two-player dice challenge of luck and risk! Each player takes turns rolling two dice. The total of the roll is added to that player’s score unless a one is rolled. If this happens, this player's turn is over, and it is the other player’s turn.


After each roll, the current player can either roll again, (assuming a "one" was not rolled) or play it safe and pass the turn to their opponent. The first player to reach 30 points or more wins!

But beware: if you roll two ones (also known as snake eyes) your entire score resets to zero. Ouch!
